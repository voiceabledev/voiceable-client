Operators ship voice AI faster when they treat agents like **systems**, not demos. Use this lightweight checklist before you scale traffic.

## 1. Purpose and boundaries

| Question | Why it matters |
|----------|----------------|
| What job does the agent own end-to-end? | Scope creep breaks transcripts and KPIs. |
| When must it escalate to a human? | Clear rules reduce liability and angry callers. |

## 2. Disclosure and consent

State clearly that the caller is speaking with an **automated system** where regulations or carriers expect it. Keep the line short and repeatable.

## 3. Measurable outcomes

Track **completion**, **escalation rate**, and **cost per successful outcome** — not just call volume. Tie definitions to business vocabulary (booked meeting, qualified lead, ticket opened).

## 4. Knowledge and tools

Ground answers in **approved sources** and restrict tools to the smallest set that achieves the job. Fewer tools means fewer failure modes.
